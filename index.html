About Me

I am a Product Management Professional with over 13 years of experience as a customer and user advocate that has worked in product support, delivery, analysis and management in digital and SaaS settings. Also my experience as an entrepreneur gives me the appreciation and humility to take ownership of all that I do and drives my passion to get things done.

My areas of expertise include SDLC, waterfall and agile (scrum/kanban) product development, management of SaaS, integrations and implementations between complex systems, working with distributed domestic, offshore and nearshore teams (Prague, Russia, India and Mexico), and incorporating digital trends and emerging technologies into solutions. I have the proven ability to learn complex technologies quickly and effectively apply new knowledge. Detailed oriented with strong problem-solving, excellent internal and external customer management, multi-tasking skills, project management, communication, teamwork skills and process improvement. I place an importance on building relationships across an organization and with my network.
